Ingredients:
============

* 3 Avacados
* 2 Tomatoes
* 1 Jalepeno
* 1 Onion
* 1 clove Garlic minced
* 1 Lime

Directions:
============

1. Dice and combine avacados, tomatoes, jalepeno, onion, and garlic in a bowl
2. Add the juice of one lime to the bowl
3. Mix until the desired consistency is reached
